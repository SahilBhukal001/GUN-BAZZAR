DSR-Precision DSR 50 Sniper Rifle

DSR-Precision DSR 50 Sniper Rifle stands at the top among the deadliest guns. DSR-Precision GmbH in Germany manufactures DSR-Precision DSR 50 Sniper Rifle. It has a range of 1500 m, and it fires .50 calibre. It is equipped with free-floating barrel technology. One quality that makes this gun even more dangerous is that it is highly adjustable and short in length. It has a holder in the front, which makes it even faster by reducing the reload time.

Heckler and Koch, HK 416 Assault Rifle

Heckler and Koch made another killing machine. HK 416 is said to be a modernized form of American M4. HK 416 is a 5.56mm (here ‘mm’ means ‘millimetre) assault rifle that can fire 700 to 900 rounds per minute with an effective range of 600 meters. This gun uses a short-stroke gas piston system. It has been used extensively during the war in Afghanistan, the Iraq war, and the Turkish-Kurdish conflict , HK 416 Assault Rifles to kill dangerous man Osama Bin Laden in 2011.

Thompson M1921

Thompson M1921 is also known as “Tommy Gun.” It was designed in 1919, and it is named after the developer, General John Taliaferro Thompson. It is neither a pistol nor a complete machine gun. This gun is used by many police officials, soldiers, and security agencies because of its accuracy and lightweight. It weighs 4.69 kg and can fire 1000 rounds per minute, and it has an effective range of 100-150m.Thompson M1921 has a .45ACP calibre.This gun uses a short-stroke gas piston system,used by many police officials

Heckler and Koch, MG4 MG 43

Hecker and Koch, a German company, created this beast. It is a 5.56 mm belt-fed machine gun. It is highly accurate, which is why it is called Perfect Killer; it never misses the target. It was developed in 1990 but gained popularity in 2000. MG4 MG 43 Machine Gun can shoot 775 to 885 rounds per minute. MG4 MG 43 Machine Gun has an effective range of 1000 meters. It can work fine in adverse situations, and it is powerful enough to cause irrecoverable damage
